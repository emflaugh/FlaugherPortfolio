Avocado Breakfast Toast

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1 Hardboiled Egg, thinly sliced
  • 1 slice Wheat Bread, toasted
  • 1 tbsp. lime juice
  • 2 tbsp. diced red onion
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • Salt
  • Fresh Ground Pepper
  • 1 tsp. chives, chopped

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Smash the avocado in a small bowl.
  2. Add lime juice, diced onion, cumin, salt and pepper and mix well.
  3. Spread avocado mixture on the toasted bread.
  4. Top with egg slices.
  5. Garnish with chopped chives and pepper.
  6. Enjoy!
